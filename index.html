California's Measles Vaccination Coverage

To protect against measles outbreaks, experts recommend that at least 95% of a community receive the MMR vaccine. During the
2018-2019 school year, 866 of California's 6,523 reporting elementary schools had kindergarten MMR coverage below this threshold.
